A Plus Genic

Online Tutoring Center

A Plus Genic is an online tutoring center dedicated to delivering engaging and effective learning experiences for students. Through live Zoom classes, experienced tutors, and small class sizes, we create an interactive learning environment where every student has the opportunity to ask questions, participate actively, and receive the guidance they need to succeed.

Whether in private or small-group classes, our lessons are designed to combine the flexibility of online learning with the interaction and support of an in-person classroom. At A Plus Genic, we believe that meaningful learning happens when students feel connected, confident, and inspired to achieve their academic goals.

What Sets Us Apart

Experienced Educators

Our tutors have over 7 years of teaching experience across both national and international curricula. This extensive background equips students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to excel academically in any learning environment.

Highly Qualified Tutors

Many of our tutors hold Master's degrees from leading public universities in Indonesia. They combine strong academic backgrounds with extensive teaching expertise, ensuring high-quality instruction and deep subject matter knowledge for every student.

Interactive Online Learning

Our live Zoom classes are designed to be engaging, interactive, and highly collaborative. We create a dynamic learning experience that feels just as personal and effective as an in-person classroom, with real-time discussions and active student participation.

Private & Small-Group Classes

We offer both one-on-one tutoring and small-group classes to suit different learning preferences. This flexible approach ensures every student receives meaningful guidance, encourages active participation, and provides the personalized support necessary for academic success.

Flexible Learning Schedule

We understand that every student has different commitments. Whenever possible, we provide flexible scheduling to help students balance their studies with school and extracurricular activities.
